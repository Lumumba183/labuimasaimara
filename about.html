About Labui Maasaimara

Two decades of crafting extraordinary safari experiences in the heart of Kenya

Our Story

Rooted in the Land, Guided by Tradition

Labui Maasaimara Safaris & Car Hire was born from a deep love for the Maasai Mara and a desire to share its wonders with the world. Founded by local Maasai guides who grew up tracking wildlife across these ancient plains, our company brings an authentic, intimate perspective to every safari we lead.

What started as a single open-sided Land Cruiser and a handful of loyal clients has grown into one of Kenya's most respected safari operators — yet we've never lost the personal touch that makes every Labui safari feel like a journey with family.

Our name "Labui" carries special meaning in the Maasai language, reflecting our commitment to preserving the cultural heritage and natural treasures of this extraordinary landscape for generations to come.

Our Mission

Creating Life-Changing Wildlife Experiences

Our mission is simple yet profound: to connect people with the wild in ways that inspire wonder, foster conservation, and support local communities. We believe that when you experience the raw beauty of the Maasai Mara — the thunder of hooves during the Great Migration, the silent gaze of a leopard at dusk, the warmth of a Maasai welcome — you become an ambassador for its protection.

Every safari we design is built around three pillars: exceptional wildlife encounters, authentic cultural immersion, and sustainable tourism practices that ensure the Mara's ecosystems thrive for future generations.

We don't just show you animals. We help you understand the intricate web of life that makes the Maasai Mara one of the most biodiverse and spiritually significant places on Earth.

Our Edge

What Sets Us Apart

Locally Owned & Operated

We're not a foreign corporation — we're a Kenyan family business born in Narok, at the doorstep of the Maasai Mara. Your booking directly supports the local community.

Expert Maasai Guides

Our guides are certified professionals with deep knowledge of wildlife behavior, tracking skills passed down through generations, and an instinct for finding the Big Five.

Premium Fleet

Our open-sided Toyota Land Cruisers are custom-modified for safari viewing, regularly maintained, and equipped with charging ports, coolers, and first-aid kits.

Personalized Service

No two safaris are alike. We listen to your interests — whether it's big cats, bird watching, or photography — and craft a bespoke itinerary just for you.

Eco-Conscious Tourism

We follow strict eco-guidelines, minimize our environmental footprint, and partner with conservancies that protect wildlife corridors and habitat.
