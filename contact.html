Common Questions

Quick Booking FAQ

How do I book a safari?

The easiest way is to message us on WhatsApp at +254 701 125 189. Tell us what you're interested in, your travel dates, and group size, and we'll send you a custom itinerary and quote.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we typically require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking, with the balance due on arrival. We accept bank transfer, M-Pesa, and cash.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom safaris. Whether you want a photography-focused trip, a family-friendly adventure, or a multi-park circuit, we'll design it around your preferences.

What's included in your safari packages?

Our packages typically include open-side 4x4 game drives, accommodation, meals, park entrance fees, professional Maasai guide, and pickup/drop-off. Each package varies, so we'll send you a detailed inclusions list.

Is it safe to travel to Kenya?

Yes! Kenya's national parks and reserves are extremely safe for tourists. Our guides are trained in safety protocols, and our vehicles are equipped with first-aid kits and communication radios.

What should I pack for a safari?

Neutral-colored clothing (khaki, olive, brown), a warm jacket for early mornings, sunscreen, a hat, binoculars, camera with zoom lens, and comfortable closed-toe shoes. We'll send you a complete packing list when you book!
