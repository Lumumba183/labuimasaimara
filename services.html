Maasai Mara Safari Package

Our signature full ground package delivers the ultimate Maasai Mara experience. Includes open-sided 4x4 game drives, comfortable accommodation, all meals, park entrance fees, and the expertise of our finest Maasai guides. Available as 2-day, 3-day, or extended safaris. Witness the Great Migration, spot the Big Five, and create memories that last forever.

Includes: Accommodation, meals, game drives, park fees, guide, transport

Amboseli Safari Package

Journey to Amboseli National Park, home to Kenya's largest elephant herds and the breathtaking backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro. Our Amboseli package includes open jeep game drives across the park's dusty plains, accommodation with Kilimanjaro views, all meals, and park fees. Spot elephants, lions, cheetahs, buffalo, and over 400 bird species.

Includes: Transport from Nairobi, lodge stay, meals, game drives, park fees

Naivasha & Nakuru Package

Combine two spectacular Rift Valley lakes in one incredible trip. Visit Lake Nakuru to see thousands of pink flamingos, white and black rhinos, lions, and leopards. Then explore Lake Naivasha with a scenic boat ride among hippos and hundreds of bird species. Includes game drives, boat safari, accommodation, meals, and all park fees.

Includes: Both parks entry, boat ride, accommodation, meals, transport

Photography Safari

Designed specifically for wildlife photographers, this specialized safari focuses on positioning, lighting, and timing. Our guides understand photography needs — they know the best angles for lion portraits, where to catch golden hour light on elephants, and how to approach birds for that perfect shot. Extended time at sightings, quiet approach techniques, and flexible scheduling.

Includes: Photography guide, extended game drives, positioning expertise

Sundowner Experience

There's nothing quite like a sundowner in the African bush. As the sun dips below the Mara horizon, painting the sky in shades of gold, amber, and violet, you'll enjoy chilled drinks and canapes at a specially chosen vantage point. Watch wildlife silhouetted against the sunset while toasting to another perfect day in paradise.

Includes: Drinks, snacks, scenic location, return transport

Breakfast in the Bush

Rise with the African sun and enjoy a gourmet breakfast served in the heart of the savanna. Freshly brewed Kenyan coffee, tropical fruits, eggs made to order, and warm pastries — all while zebra, giraffe, and antelope graze in the distance. A truly magical start to your day that engages every sense and creates memories you'll treasure forever.

Includes: Full breakfast, setup in scenic location, hot beverages
